Gabriella Masa-Horvath

A Short Bio

Hi everyone, I’m Gabriella Masa-Horvath, I’m a marriage coach and an emotional management trainer based in Paris, who helps mamas make their marriage better, not with changing their husbands, but more with teaching them how to stay centered and say YES to their desires.

Before starting helping people make their marriage better, I did my own hero’s journey from being forced to leave my country due to the civil war when I was 16, through overcoming serious addictions to establishing a corporate career and finally arrive to living the life I was meant to live. I coach my clients how to overcome adversities in their lives, how to use their suffering to grow, and hope to inspire people all around the world by giving an example of what is possible. 

I was born and raised in Ex-Yugoslavia, but as I mentioned before, due to the war we moved to Hungary, where I lived the next 23 years of my life. This is my second marriage I live in, which has given me opportunity to become a mother and a step-mother as well. I love having this huge, complicated family, I just feel richer and more resilient.

My number one favorite thing is my son. He is 7 years old and he is the most perfect man ever. My second favorite passion is my work and also dancing. I often look at these 2 as one, because I can’t imagine my life without them. And of course the last but not least is my husband and his friendship to me. With him being an artist, gives me an unaccepted benefit of being his muse. Experiencing this attribute helped me to become the coach I am today.
 
I love my family, I love creating with others, I love art, movies, the sea, the sun, philosophy, anything about psychology and of course dancing.
