ABOUT

Gabriella Masa-Horvath

Gabriella is a life coach and an emotional management trainer who helps her clients to get out of that pit when they hit the bottom.

Before starting helping people make their life better, Gabriella did her own hero’s journey from being forced to leave her country due to the civil war, through overcoming heroin addiction to establishing a corporate career and finally arrive to living the life she was meant to live. She coaches expat moms to stay centered in their transition to the new life they have chosen. To be who they were meant to be and live as brave as they are.

Gabriella enjoys her family, being a muse to her artist husband and of course dancing.
